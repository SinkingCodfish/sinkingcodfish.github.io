A first-person psychological horror puzzle game

where the player must die!




CONCEPT


Players usually play to survive, to achieve a goal. But what if they played to die?


In a first-person psychological horror adventure inspired by the classic claymation style, we want death to be a clear goal for the player, but with a journey built around puzzles that make their deaths more creative (some even unexpected).

Our only way to escape will be to go through it over and over again. Besides, this had always been our dream. It was our choice from the beginning: to die. To die endlessly. But every dream turns into a nightmare in the blink of an eye, in the swallow of saliva, in a glance misunderstood. And death—it doesn’t come just once.
