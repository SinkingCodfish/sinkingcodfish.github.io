Our Games



Project S.T.O.R.M.



Gear up for a heart-pounding multiplayer adventure with Project S.T.O.R.M.! Dive into the pulse-pounding world of this co-op puzzle escape-room game, where horror, suspense, and mystical vibes collide.

Embark on a journey into the mysterious depths of an underwater research facility, shrouded in secrecy. Little do you know, you’re about to become a pawn in mind-bending experiments. Each playthrough is a fresh test, unraveling the true nature of these experiments through sneaky collectibles.

Join forces with your crew for cooperative puzzle-solving, deciphering cryptic instructions and unveiling the facility’s enigmatic secrets. Get ready for an adrenaline-packed experience that will keep you on the edge of your seat!





Mirage Blocks



We present you the ultimate block party with a twist of mirror magic!

Our block puzzle game is not your average Joe – it’s like playing with blocks in a funhouse. Double the boards, double the fun! Toss those blocks on either side of the mirror, watch them flip, flop, and dazzle your brain. Can’t find a spot for a piece? No worries, give them a shuffle dance!

Score big by filling lines and columns – it’s not just a puzzle, it’s a block bonanza! Cross those lines like a puzzle ninja for some mind-blasting action. Get ready to flex your brain muscles and laugh your way to the highest score. Let the block party begin!







Music Ark

This music based game is the best chill out game to play on a daily basis, while meeting new music artists!

Every day, a new song will be available to you, you have 5 attempts to unlock it!

Avoid all the enemies and get the high score by surviving, defeating enemies and painting the map. Surviving until the song ends unlocks it permanently for you to challenge at will in your collection.

Your chosen instruments strike to the beat, the faster the music the faster your attacks. Your path and instruments will paint the ground and reveal a colorful background to accompany you on this adventure. So choose your instruments, beat the song and collect them all, good luck!



So, are you ready to paint your way to musical victory? Grab your brush, cue up your favorite indie track, and let the Artful Vibes begin!





Fall Fish - Get down to rock bottom!



In this fishy platform game, fall like your life depends on it… Because it does!

Get to your high score by avoiding dangers, flopping from platform to platform, gathering useful collectibles and staying alive for as long as possible.

You would also do well to remember that while going down far and collecting stuff is good for your score, you have oxygen levels to maintain! Good luck, hope you hit rock bottom!





Romano´s Mine



Dive into the serious fun of our city sim mobile game designed to enlighten and entertain! Forget the usual mobile games; ours has a mission – to educate you about the intricate world of lithium exploration and refinement. Brace yourself for an environmental rollercoaster as you learn how these processes can shape your city and the surrounding areas.

Build, refine, and explore the lithium life! Uncover the environmental impacts and discover how it ripples through the population and the neighborhoods. This isn’t just a game; it’s an education in your pocket. So, buckle up, because learning about lithium has never been this entertaining!




Warlock



Dive into the wildest mobile multiplayer battle arena with a zoo-tastic twist!

Welcome to a 4-player showdown where each contender commands a unique animal armed with special abilities. The arena shrinks as time ticks away, and the stakes get wilder! Stray outside the shrinking battleground or take a beating from your opponents, and you’ll be kissing your life points goodbye. It’s a wild safari of survival, and the last critter standing claims victory!

Ready to unleash your inner animal instincts and rule the arena? Let the fur fly!
